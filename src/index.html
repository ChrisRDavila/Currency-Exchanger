      For country code:
      Currency: AED (for Emirati Dirham from UAE)

      For amount in US Dollars
      Amount in USD: 10.00   (for ten United States dollars)
      Amount in USD: .50     (for fifty United States cents)
      Amount in USD: 1000000 (for one million United States dollars)
